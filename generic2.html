Summary
As a group I think we were all happy with how we worked together and contributed to the assignment. We all have different university subjects that we are also completing other assignments and tasks on so it was a juggle for everyone to find time to complete this assignment. It was very beneficial to have sorted out in the tutorial class who was responsible for what, as this means there wasn’t much confusion on what had to be done for each person. By spending the extra time in class to create a table with each part of the assignment broken down into who does what, we created a list for each person on what their parts were for the assignment. By splitting the workload, we could tackle this large task by completing bit by bit and checking off the list of things to do for each person. Each members interest in IT varied and drove them when completing the IT technology written pieces on an area they were interested in. Group members provided feedback and communicated with each other well and everyone completed their section of the assignment before the due date.
